Your guide and storyteller

For him, guiding is not simply a job; it's a way of sharing the Ireland he knows and loves. Whether travelling along rugged coastlines, through ancient landscapes, or into charming villages, he brings each location to life with stories, local insights, and a deep appreciation for the people and events that have shaped this remarkable country.

Guests often comment on his natural storytelling ability and the genuine passion he brings to every tour. His engaging narratives transform landmarks into living history, creating memorable experiences that stay with visitors long after they return home.

Safety and comfort are always at the forefront of every journey. As a professional driver, he encourages guests to relax, enjoy the scenery, and make the most of every stop along the way. He believes that travel should never be rushed; some of the best memories are made when you take time to pause, explore, and appreciate the beauty around you.

His Grá for Ireland

His Grá (love) for the Irish language, heritage, and traditions adds a unique dimension to every tour, offering visitors a deeper connection to the heart and soul of Ireland.

We created these tour-guiding experiences to share that passion with travellers from around the world. Whether you're discovering Ireland for the first time or returning to explore more, we hope our tours inspire the same sense of wonder, curiosity, and appreciation that has inspired us for years.

Come along, hear the stories, discover the history, and experience the magic of Ireland — one journey at a time.

About us — the family behind MacÉireann

What started as a simple idea between family members has grown into a project we are incredibly proud to share with the world.

We are a team of four individuals, connected not only by family ties but by a shared passion for travel, discovery, and storytelling. Coming from diverse backgrounds and bringing different experiences, skills, and perspectives to the table, we each played a unique role in turning an idea into reality.

As passionate world travellers ourselves, we've always believed that the best journeys are those that allow you to connect with a place, its people, and its stories. Inspired by our grá (love) of Ireland and the breathtaking beauty of the Wild Atlantic Way, we set out to create an experience that would help visitors explore this remarkable coastline in a meaningful and memorable way.

Our audio-guided tour app was born from that vision. What began as conversations around a table gradually evolved into countless hours of research, planning, recording, testing, and learning. Along the way, we discovered not only more about Ireland's history, culture, and hidden gems, but also more about each other. Every challenge helped us appreciate the individual strengths, talents, and creativity that each person brings to the company.

Creating this app has been a journey in itself — one filled with learning, collaboration, laughter, and a genuine belief in what we are building. We are passionate about delivering an experience that allows travellers to explore at their own pace while uncovering the stories, landscapes, and heritage that make the Wild Atlantic Way so special.

Today, that same passion continues to drive us forward. We are proud to share our love of Ireland with visitors from around the world and hope our tours inspire curiosity, adventure, and unforgettable memories along one of the world's most spectacular coastal routes.

From our family to yours, welcome to the journey.
