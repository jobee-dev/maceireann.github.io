MacÉireann ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is operated by Yarn Point Tours Ltd ("the Company"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the App, how we use and share it, the choices you have, and how we keep it secure.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Note on scope: Sections 1–13 describe how we handle data within the MacÉireann mobile app. Section 14 separately explains how our website at maceireann.ie uses cookies, analytics, and newsletter sign-ups. The website is distinct from the app — it does not require an account or access your device location.

Data controller Yarn Point Tours Ltd

2. Summary

  • The App provides self-guided audio driving tours of Ireland, with GPS location triggering of tour audio and turn-by-turn navigation.
  • We collect the information you provide when you create an account (e.g. email address) and complete onboarding (e.g. display name and your preferred/home airport).
  • We use your device location, including in the background while a tour is active, to play the right audio at the right place and to provide navigation.
  • We use trusted third-party providers — Supabase (accounts and data storage), RevenueCat (subscriptions and purchases), Mapbox (maps and navigation), and the Apple App Store / Google Play (purchases) — to operate the App.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Account information. When you sign up or sign in, we collect your email address and authentication credentials (managed securely by our authentication provider, Supabase).
  • Profile / onboarding information. During onboarding you may provide a display name and your preferred or home airport, and set tour preferences (e.g. traveler type and topics of interest). This helps us personalize tour recommendations.
  • Reviews and support communications. If you submit a tour review or contact us (e.g. by email), we collect the information you choose to share, such as an author name and review text.

3.2 Information collected automatically

  • Location data. With your permission, the App accesses your device's precise GPS location. Location is used to:
    • automatically trigger tour audio as you approach points of interest;
    • provide turn-by-turn navigation along tour routes; and
    • show your position on the map.

    The App requests "while using" and "always/background" location access so that audio and navigation continue to work while the App is in the background and your screen is off during a drive. You can change or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings (see Section 8).

  • Purchase and subscription data. When you buy a subscription or unlock tours, our purchases provider (RevenueCat) and the relevant app store process the transaction and provide us with information about your entitlements and subscription status. We do not receive or store your full payment-card details — these are handled by Apple or Google.
  • Device and technical information. To operate, secure, and troubleshoot the App, we may collect technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, language/region, and a unique app/device identifier.
  • App activity. We may collect limited information about how you use the App, such as which tours you start, download, or complete, to improve the experience.

3.3 Information stored on your device

Some data — such as your authentication session, downloaded/offline tour content, and app preferences — is stored locally on your device (using secure on-device storage). This data stays on your device unless it needs to sync with our services.

4. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your account and authenticate you;
  • deliver the core features of the App — playing location-triggered audio, providing navigation, and showing maps;
  • personalize tour recommendations based on your preferences (e.g. home airport);
  • process and validate subscriptions and purchases and grant the corresponding access;
  • provide customer support and respond to your enquiries;
  • maintain the security, reliability, and performance of the App, including diagnosing and fixing problems; and
  • comply with our legal obligations.

5. Legal bases for processing (EEA / UK users)

Where the GDPR (or UK GDPR) applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the App and the features you request (e.g. accounts, navigation, audio, purchases).
  • Consent — for access to your device location, which you grant through the operating system permission prompt. You may withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure, maintain, and improve the App, where these interests are not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal obligation — where we must process data to comply with the law.

8. Your choices and rights

8.1 Location permission

You can grant, limit, or revoke the App's access to your location at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → MacÉireann
  • Android: Settings → Apps → MacÉireann → Permissions → Location

Note that disabling location will prevent location-triggered audio and navigation from working.

8.2 Your data-protection rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • delete your data ("right to erasure");
  • restrict or object to certain processing;
  • request portability of your data; and
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@maceireann.ie. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority — in Ireland, the Data Protection Commission (https://www.dataprotection.ie).

8.3 Deleting your account

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by emailing info@maceireann.ie. Some information may be retained where required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes (see Section 9).

9. Data retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy:

  • Account and profile data — for as long as your account is active, and then deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period after account closure.
  • Purchase records — retained as required for tax, accounting, and legal compliance.
  • Location data — used in real time to provide the tour and navigation features and is not retained as a long-term location history by the App beyond what is needed to operate these features.

10. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encryption in transit, secure on-device storage of sensitive data such as authentication sessions, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

14. Our website (maceireann.ie)

This section applies specifically to our website at maceireann.ie, which is separate from the MacÉireann mobile app described in the rest of this policy. The website is an information and marketing site: you do not need an account to use it, and it does not access your device location.

14.1 Cookies and analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the website — for example, which tour pages are popular and how people arrive — so that we can improve it. This is the only analytics or cookie technology we use on the website.

Google Analytics may set cookies and collect information such as the pages you view, your approximate location (country or region, derived from your IP address, which is not stored in full), device and browser type, and the website that referred you. This information is pseudonymous and is not used to identify you personally.

We use Google's Consent Mode to keep this consent-based:

  • On your first visit, a cookie banner asks whether you accept analytics cookies. No analytics cookies are set unless you choose "Accept."
  • If you decline — or have not yet chosen — Google Analytics runs in a cookieless mode that provides us with limited, aggregated measurement only.
  • You can change your choice at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the website footer, which reopens the banner.

Google acts as our analytics provider (processor) for the website. To learn more, see Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and how Google uses information from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. Storage that is strictly necessary for the website and for remembering your cookie choice is always enabled.

14.2 Newsletter sign-up

If you enter your email address in a sign-up form on the website, we use it to send you trip-planning tips and news about new tours. We process this on the basis of your consent, and you can unsubscribe or ask us to delete your address at any time by emailing info@maceireann.ie.

14.3 Website hosting

The website is hosted on GitHub Pages, and newsletter sign-ups are handled through a service operated on Cloudflare. To deliver and secure the website, these providers process limited technical request data (such as your IP address and browser information) under their own privacy policies.

14.4 Legal basis (EEA / UK visitors)

For website visitors in the EEA or UK, we rely on your consent for analytics cookies and for newsletter sign-up, and on our legitimate interest in operating and securing the website for strictly necessary functions. You can withdraw your consent at any time as described above. The rights set out in Section 8.2, and the right to complain to a data-protection authority, apply equally to data collected through the website.
